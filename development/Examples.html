Examples¶

There are several examples available that cover different setups. These examples are a good starting point to get to know Testbeam Analysis.

ATLAS FE-I4 telescope¶

The FE-I4 telescope data example shows how to run a full analysis on data taken with a FE-I4 telescope.

Note

Only prealignment is done here, since the telescope data is too coarse to profit from an aligment step. The data was recorded at DESY with pyBar. The telescope consists of 6 DUTs with ~ 2 cm distance between the planes. Only the first two and last two planes were taken here. The first and last plane were IBL n-in-n planar sensors and the 2 devices in the center 3D CNM/FBK sensors.
